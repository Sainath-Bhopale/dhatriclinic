About Dharti

Rooted in Tradition, Guided by Compassion

At Dharti Ayurvedic Eye Clinic, we believe the eyes are not merely organs of sight — they are the windows to your entire constitution. Our practice draws from the rich Shalakya Tantra branch of Ayurveda, the ancient science of eye care, to address conditions modern medicine often manages but rarely resolves.

Every patient receives a thorough Prakriti analysis, dosha assessment, and a bespoke treatment plan combining classical therapies with lifestyle guidance.

  • Comprehensive Ayurvedic eye examinations
  • Authentic Panchakarma detox therapies
  • Custom herbal preparations & Rasayanas
  • Yoga & Trataka (eye gazing) practices
  • Dietary & Ahara guidance for eye health
  • Treatment for glaucoma, cataracts & dry eyes

What We Offer

Specialised Ayurvedic Eye Services

Each therapy is delivered by a trained Ayurvedic physician in a calm, healing environment using only authentic, ethically sourced ingredients.

👁️

Netra Tarpana

Medicated ghee is retained in a dough dam around the eye, deeply nourishing the optic nerve and relieving dryness, strain, and early-stage vision loss.

🌊

Netra Dhara

A continuous stream of herbal decoction is poured over open eyes to cleanse, cool, and purify ocular tissues, ideal for conjunctivitis and redness.

🖤

Anjana Therapy

Application of medicated collyrium (kajal) prepared from classical Ayurvedic recipes to cleanse the eye channels and strengthen sight.

💧

Aschyotana

Precise instillation of herbal eye drops — the Ayurvedic equivalent of medicated drops — tailored to your doshic imbalance.

🍃

Bidalaka

Application of a medicinal herbal paste over closed eyelids to address inflammation, early cataract, and ptosis through transdermal absorption.

🔥

Shirodhara

A continuous stream of warm herbal oil poured on the forehead to calm the nervous system, relieving stress-related vision problems and migraines.
